ROUTINES

Over the past few years, skincare has grown from a niche routine into a mainstream cultural phenomenon, blending self-care with self-expression. People are drawn not only to the promise of healthier, glowing skin but also to the ritualistic, calming experience that daily skincare provides. A moment of mindfulness in an otherwise hectic world. Skincare has become a reflection of culture itself, with trends, products, and routines influenced by social media, global beauty standards, and the desire for personal wellness. Its more than a cosmetic practice, its a way for individuals to connect with themselves, express identity, and engage in a shared cultural conversation about care, confidence, and beauty.
